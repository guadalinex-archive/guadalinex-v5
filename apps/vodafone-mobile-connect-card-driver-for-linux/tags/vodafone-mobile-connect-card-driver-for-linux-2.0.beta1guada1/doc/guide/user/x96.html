3. Controlling usage

You can control your usage toggling on the Usage button. It will show you a panel in what you will see on one side some information about your connections along the current month and session and on the other side a pair of bar graphs with your usage of 3G and GPRS connections.

Usage

The information you can get here is:

  • The month whose information is showed

  • The transferred data in the month. Using GPRS and 3G, and se sum of both.

  • The same data of the current session

  • There is also another tab to watch the same information of the last month.

In the usage tab of preferences window, you can choose to receive a notification if a threshold is reached, so you can control your consumption.
